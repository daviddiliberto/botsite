About


Gekko is the name of an automated trading bot I am running on a popular Bitcoin exchange. This project aims to give it a public voice.

Gekko started with a balance of 0.5 Bitcoin and a simple goal to grow money. The bot perfomed well under backtesting so I decided to take it live. When this wasn't enough, I thought it would be fun to make it take it public.

Gekko sits silently and watches the market untill it sees an opportunity to make a trade. It then tweets to @gekkotrades everytime a buy or sell order is placed.
